Introducing Afrik Hotel: Your Trusted Destination For Exceptional Hospitality

In September 2015, a group of forward-thinking Somali entrepreneurs established Afrik Hotel and Restaurant, driven by the growing hospitality landscape in the country. Committed to setting new standards in luxury accommodation and dining, Afrik Hotel strives to lead the hospitality sector and expand its presence nationwide. Your Safety is Our Priority: At Afrik Hotel, ensuring your safety and well-being is paramount. Our security team, extensively trained by international experts and licensed by the Ministry of Internal Security, maintains round-the-clock surveillance and stringent access controls. Rest assured, your stay at Afrik Hotel is both secure and peaceful.
